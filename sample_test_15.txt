Branch 1: Perceiving Emotions

Question 1

Scenario & Question: [Age 15] Jordan walked into the school cafeteria and saw their friend group huddled together, laughing and looking at someone's phone. When Jordan approached, the group suddenly stopped talking and exchanged quick glances. One friend said, "Oh hey, Jordan," in a slightly forced, flat tone while others looked away. Which emotions is Jordan most likely experiencing in this moment?

Options:
A) Excitement and joy
B) Confusion and hurt
C) Anger and aggression
D) Indifference and calm
E) Mild curiosity

Expert Consensus Scores: A: 1, B: 5, C: 2, D: 1, E: 3

Question 2

Scenario & Question: [Age 15] Look at the image of the person. Their eyebrows are pulled slightly together and upward, their mouth is turned down at the corners, and they are looking down and away from others. Their shoulders are slightly slumped. Which of the following emotions is this person most likely feeling?

Options:
A) Happiness and excitement
B) Disgust and anger
C) Sadness and disappointment
D) Fear and surprise
E) Confidence and pride

Expert Consensus Scores: A: 1, B: 2, C: 5, D: 3, E: 1

Question 3

Scenario & Question: [Age 15] Morgan spent weeks preparing a presentation for a class competition. Right after finishing the presentation, the audience was silent for a moment before starting to clap. Morgan walked off the stage, took a deep breath, and said to a friend, "Well, I'm just glad it's over," while avoiding eye contact and fidgeting with their hands. Which emotions is Morgan most likely experiencing?

Options:
A) Pure relief and satisfaction
B) Disappointment and anxiety about their performance
C) Boredom and indifference
D) Anger towards the audience
E) Pride and excitement for the next opportunity

Expert Consensus Scores: A: 2, B: 5, C: 1, D: 1, E: 3
